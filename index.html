  • Employee: Can participate in meetings, chat, calls, screen sharing, and manage assigned tasks

    • 2. Communication Tools :

  • Meetings: Schedule and conduct meetings with integrated video call and screen-sharing capabilities.

  • Task Assignment: Managers can assign tasks to employees with specified deadlines.

  • Notifications: Automatic notifications to managers if tasks are completed or missed by the deadline.

    • 4. Theming and Responsiveness :

  • Dark and Light Themes: Users can toggle between dark and light modes based on their preference.

  • Responsive Design: Ensures optimal user experience across various devices and screen sizes.

    • 5. Public View and User Panels:

  • Public View Page: Accessible information for visitors and prospective clients.

  • User Panels: Dedicated panels for admin, managers, and employees, tailored to their specific needs and functionalities.
